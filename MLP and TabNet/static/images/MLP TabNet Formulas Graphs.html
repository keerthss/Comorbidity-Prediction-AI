a(l)=f(W(l)a(l1)+b(l))a^{(l)} = f(W^{(l)}a^{(l-1)} + b^{(l)})a(l)=f(W(l)a(l1)+b(l))

M(i)=Sparsemax(P(i)AttentiveTransform(X))M^{(i)} = \text{Sparsemax}(P^{(i)} \cdot \text{AttentiveTransform}(X))M(i)=Sparsemax(P(i)AttentiveTransform(X))

Where:

  • M(i)M^{(i)}M(i) is the feature mask at decision step iii,

  • P(i)P^{(i)}P(i) is the prior scale that updates after each step,
