Diabetes: Understanding the Condition & Predicting the Risk

March 23, 2025 by Health Insights

Diabetes is a chronic metabolic disorder that affects how the body processes blood sugar (glucose). High blood sugar levels over time can lead to serious complications affecting the heart, kidneys, nerves, and eyes. But with early prediction and proper management, diabetes-related complications can be prevented.

Types of Diabetes

  • Type 1 Diabetes: An autoimmune condition where the body attacks insulin-producing cells. It requires insulin therapy.
  • Type 2 Diabetes: The most common type, often linked to lifestyle, genetics, and insulin resistance. It can be managed with diet, exercise, and medication.
  • Gestational Diabetes: Occurs during pregnancy and increases the risk of developing Type 2 diabetes later.
  • Prediabetes: A warning stage where blood sugar levels are higher than normal but not yet in the diabetic range. Early lifestyle changes can reverse it.
